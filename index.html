Our Story

Tradition Met By Fire.

Primo Taglio was born out of a singular obsession: mastering the timeless art of wood-fired sourdough baking.

We source heritage grains, hydrate our dough for 72 hours, and fire each pizza over selected organic hickory lines inside our custom 900-degree stone chamber.
